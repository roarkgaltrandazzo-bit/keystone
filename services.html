How to choose

The right service depends on what the review finds.

If visibility is weak

Fix pipeline and CRM first.

Leadership needs a reliable view of opportunities before it can manage commercial performance.

If margin is unclear

Fix pricing and customer mix first.

More revenue will not solve the problem if the work being won is diluting profit.

If the owner is the system

Fix roles and transferability first.

The company needs standards others can follow before it can scale leadership beyond the owner.
