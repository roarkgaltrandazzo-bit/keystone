Founder | Keystone Commercial Partners | Appleton, WI

Tom has spent more than 20 years working on the commercial side of owner-led, PE-backed, and Fortune 500 businesses, inside the pricing decisions, pipeline reviews, key account conversations, and the moment when an owner realizes the business has grown past what one person can control.

He started Keystone to do inside smaller, owner-led companies what only larger companies typically have the infrastructure to do: build a commercial system that runs on structure, not on the founder.

Keystone works primarily with Wisconsin and Midwest companies that value straight talk, practical systems, and work that survives contact with the real business.

Philosophy

The work has to survive contact with the business.

A good commercial system should make decision-making clearer, not more performative. It should tell the team who owns the next step, when pricing needs approval, which accounts deserve focus, and what the pipeline actually says about future revenue.

That is why Keystone starts with the diagnostic. Before anyone builds tools, hires ahead, changes compensation, or installs another platform, the commercial structure underneath it has to be understood.

Example Outcomes
