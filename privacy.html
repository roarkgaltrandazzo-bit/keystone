Privacy

Privacy Policy

Keystone Commercial Partners collects only the information needed to respond to inquiries, schedule conversations, and provide services.

Last updated: May 19, 2026

Information We Collect

When you contact Keystone Commercial Partners through this website, email, a scheduling tool, or a form, we may collect your name, company name, role, email address, phone number, revenue range, and any information you choose to provide about your business or reason for reaching out.

How We Use Information

We use submitted information to respond to inquiries, schedule conversations, evaluate whether Keystone may be a fit, provide requested services, maintain business records, improve website communication, and follow up with relevant information related to Keystone Commercial Partners.

Website Analytics and Third-Party Tools

This website may use third-party tools for forms, scheduling, analytics, email, hosting, and basic website performance. These tools may process limited information according to their own privacy policies. Keystone does not sell personal information to advertisers.

Information Sharing

Keystone does not sell your personal information. Information may be shared only with service providers who help operate the website or deliver services, when required by law, or when reasonably necessary to protect Keystone's rights, property, or business operations.

Data Retention

Keystone keeps submitted information for as long as needed to respond to your inquiry, maintain business records, provide services, or comply with legal and administrative obligations.

Your Choices

You may request that Keystone update or delete information you previously provided, subject to reasonable business, legal, or recordkeeping requirements.

Security

Keystone uses reasonable administrative and technical safeguards to protect information submitted through the website. No website or electronic communication method can be guaranteed completely secure.

Contact

For privacy questions, contact Keystone Commercial Partners at tom@keystonecommercialpartners.com.
